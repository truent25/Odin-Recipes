Homemade Alfredo Sauce Recipe

This recipe will blow all of your friend's away with not only how simple this is, but how good it comes out! You will never look at jarred sauce after this!

Required components

  • 1/4th Cup Butter
  • 1 Cup Heavy Cream
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1-1/2 cups freshly grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Directions

  1. Melt butter in medium saucepan over medium-low.
  2. Add cream and simmer for 5 minutes
  3. Add garlic and cheese, whick quickly to heat through
  4. Stir in parsley and serve with pasta of choice!
