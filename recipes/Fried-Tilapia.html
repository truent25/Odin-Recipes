Fried Tilapia Fillets

Required Ingridients

  • 1LB Tilapia
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp old bay seasoning
  • 1 tsp granulated garlic
  • 1/2 tsp lemon zest
  • 3 eggs whisked
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil, for frying

Instructions

  1. Whisk 3 eggs in bowl, set aside. Season flour with old bay, granulated garlic and lemon zest. Whisk well
  2. Season tilapia on both sides with salt and pepper
  3. Dip tilapia in eggs, coat both sides with flour. Coat 2-3 times prior to frying
  4. Fry for 2-3 minutes on each side and enjoy! Serve with lemon juice and hot sauce
