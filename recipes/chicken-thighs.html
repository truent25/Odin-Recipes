Chicken Thigh Recipe

Required Ingridients/Components

  • 4 Skin on, Bone in, Chicken Thighs
  • Seasoning's of Choice
  • Meat Thermometer
  • Zip Lock Bag
  • Air Fryer or Conventional Oven

How to prepare:

  1. Begin by Preheating Air Fryer to 375F
  2. Pat Chicken Thighs Dry and Cut Off Any Excess Skin
  3. In Ziplock Bag, Mix Chicken Thighs with Seasonings of Choice
  4. Place Chicken Thighs in Air Fryer Skin Side Down, Let Cook for 12 Minutes
  5. Flip Thighs Skin Side Up, Cook for 10 More minutes
  6. Thighs Are Done When Internal Temp is 165F
